# World-Wide-Importers-Dashboard
The World Wide Importers Dashboard is an interactive data visualization project designed to analyze global sales, revenue, and profit performance. It provides detailed insights into sales territories, employees, cities, and products, helping businesses make informed, data-driven decisions.
The World Wide Importers Dashboard is an interactive data visualization project designed to analyze global sales, revenue, and profit performance. It provides detailed insights into sales territories, employees, cities, and products, helping businesses make informed, data-driven decisions.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze sales trends over multiple years
Identify top-performing employees and regions
Track revenue and profit distribution
Highlight best-selling products and top cities
Enable dynamic and interactive data exploration

🚀 Key Features

🌎 Sales Territory Filter – Analyze performance across regions like Far West, Great Lakes, Southeast, etc.
📅 Year & Date Filter – Explore sales trends from 2013 to 2017
👨‍💼 Sales by Employees – Rank employees based on performance
📈 Trend Analysis – Monthly sales trends over multiple years
🏙️ Top 10 Cities – Identify high-performing cities
📦 Top Selling Products – Discover best-selling items
💰 Revenue & Profit Analysis – Compare revenue and profit by employees
🥧 Profit by Territory – Visual breakdown of profit contribution by region

📊 Visualizations Used

Bar Charts – Employee performance, top cities, and products
Line Chart – Sales trend over time
Pie Chart – Profit distribution by territory
Combo Chart – Revenue vs Profit comparison
Slicers/Filters – Territory, Year, and Date

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

-Microsoft Excel
-Data Cleaning & Transformation
-Data Modeling
-Data Visualization Techniques
